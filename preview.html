Our Mission

Parker Literacy Foundation is dedicated to increasing access to books for children in underserved communities. We believe literacy is the foundation for opportunity, and every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and succeed.

Our Story

What began as a small effort between siblings distributing just 60 books has grown into a mission-driven initiative. In just one year, we expanded to over 165 books distributed to children in high-poverty areas during the holiday season. Our goal is to continue growing, reaching more communities, and making literacy accessible year-round.
